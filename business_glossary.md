# Business Glossary

- **Asset**: A physical equipment item or operational component registered in the asset hierarchy.
- **Work Order**: A maintenance instruction or task tracked through the maintenance system.
- **Backlog Flag**: Indicator showing that a work order remains unresolved past expected execution timing.
- **Criticality Level**: Business-defined severity of asset importance for safety, production, or reliability.
- **Deferred Production**: Estimated lost or delayed production associated with maintenance or asset constraints.
- **Maintenance Attention Category**: Derived category used for prioritisation in reporting and AI-supported triage.
